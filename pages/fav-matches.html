Favourite United Games

Below is a list of some of my favourite Iconic United matches over the years.
These matches come from various competitions and I must have watched them to qualify for this list.

  • 1999 Champions League Final

    • This iconic game against Bayern Munich was one of the first games I properly remember watching.
    This game kickstarted my love affair with United.
    This match meant United won the first English treble! (Premier League, FA Cup and Champions League)

    Link to match report!

  • 2011 Manchester Derby

    • This game is best remembered for Wayne Rooney's famous bicycle kick goal.
    United opened the scoring through Nani, only for City to eqaulise soon after through Dzeko.
    With less then 15 minutes left Rooney popped up to score possibility the greatest derby goal of all time!

    Link to match report!
    Link to video of goal.

  • 2008 Champions League Final

    • The 2008 final was played between United and rivals Chelsea.
    The match was played in Moscow and after the 90 minutes, plus extra time, the teams couldn't be separated and penalties were required.
    The standout moment came in the penalty shoot out with Chelsea club legend and captain, John Terry, slipped when taking his penalty and hitting the post.
    This handed United the victory for their second Champions League trophy.
